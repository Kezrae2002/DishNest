Ribeye Steak

Perfectly seared ribeye steak with garlic butter and herbs Carnivore

⏱ Prep: 5 min
Cook: 12 min
🍽 2 servings
👨‍🍳 Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1-inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • Optional: flaky sea salt

Instructions

1
Remove steaks from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking.
2
Pat steaks dry and season generously with salt and pepper.
3
Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until very hot.
4
Add olive oil and place steaks in the pan.
5
Sear 3–4 minutes without moving.
6
Flip and add butter, garlic, thyme, and rosemary.
7
Baste steaks with melted herb butter while cooking.
8
Rest 5 minutes before serving. Finish with flaky salt.
