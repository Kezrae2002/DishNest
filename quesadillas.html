Quesadillas

Crispy tortillas filled with melted cheese and vegetables Quick & Easy

⏱ Prep: 5 min
Cook: 10 min
🍽 2 servings
👨‍🍳 Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/2 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Sour cream for serving
  • Salsa for serving
  • Optional: jalapeños, black beans, or corn

Instructions

1
Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
2
Spread butter on one side of each tortilla.
3
Place tortilla butter-side down in skillet.
4
Add cheese, bell pepper, and onion.
5
Fold and cook 2–3 minutes until golden.
6
Flip and cook until cheese melts.
7
Remove and cut into wedges.
8
Serve hot with sour cream and salsa.
