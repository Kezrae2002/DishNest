Grilled Chicken

Perfectly seasoned and grilled chicken breast with herbs Carnivore

⏱ Prep: 10 min
Cook: 15 min
🍽 4 servings
👨‍🍳 Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

1
Mix oil, garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
2
Coat chicken and marinate 15 minutes.
3
Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
4
Grill 6–7 minutes per side until internal temp reaches 165°F.
5
Rest for 5 minutes.
6
Slice and garnish with parsley.
7
Serve and enjoy.
