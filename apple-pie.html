Apple Pie

Classic homemade apple pie with a flaky, buttery crust Dessert

⏱ Prep: 30 min
Cook: 50 min
🍽 8 servings
👨‍🍳 Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 refrigerated pie crusts
  • 6–7 large apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Vanilla ice cream for serving

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2
Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish.
3
Toss apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
4
Pour filling into crust and dot with butter.
5
Cover with second crust and seal edges.
6
Cut slits and brush with beaten egg.
7
Bake 45–50 minutes until golden and bubbly.
8
Cool 30 minutes before serving with ice cream.
